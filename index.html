Our Activities

Hack Harris

We host a weekly policy hack night at Harris, ideal for beginners who want to learn tools for analyzing policy data and building programs and websites. All students welcome, especially if it's your first time.

Events

We host events featuring people prominent in the data and policy fields in Chicago.

Student Externships

We pair interested policy students with local Chicago organizations that need help collecting, analyzing, and visualizing data.

Career Assistance

We organize career events and alumni panels and locate part-time and full-time job opportunities for students at the intersection of data and public policy, both during and after their time in school.
